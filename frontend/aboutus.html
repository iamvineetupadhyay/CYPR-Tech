About CyberMitra

A cyber safety companion for real people.

CyberMitra is built to make security checks simple, affordable, and understandable. The goal is not to scare users with technical jargon, but to help them make safer decisions before clicking, sharing, or opening anything risky.

Mission

Protect first, explain clearly.

Every feature is designed around one idea: people should be able to understand cyber risk quickly and act with confidence.

Why we exist

Digital safety should not feel out of reach.

Most attacks begin with ordinary moments: a link in a message, a reused password, a document from an unknown sender, or a family member who is unsure what is safe. CyberMitra turns those moments into quick checks and clear next steps.
