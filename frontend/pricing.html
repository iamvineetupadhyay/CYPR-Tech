Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a long-term commitment required? ▼
No. You can subscribe monthly and cancel, upgrade, or downgrade your pricing plan at any time directly from your dashboard settings. There are absolutely no termination fees.
What makes the VAJRA Core engine faster? ▼
VAJRA is our custom concurrent threat ingestion module developed in Java 21 & Spring Boot 3. It utilizes non-blocking thread scheduling to aggregate over 1.4 Lakh threat intelligence records, caching them instantly in-memory so search lookups complete in under 1 millisecond.
How does the Brevo SMTP notifier trigger? ▼
When our scan pipelines detect that a URL or script you are checking is classified as high-risk, a background event is dispatched to trigger the Brevo SMTP engine. It immediately sends a transactional diagnostic payload with recommendations directly to your verified email address.
Are there community or open-source plans? ▼
Yes! Because CYPR is an open-source project, we are proud to offer free, complimentary Pro-tier features for active open-source developers, academic research groups, and educational organizations. Get in touch with details of your work to get set up.
