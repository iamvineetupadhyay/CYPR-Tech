Security Tips

🔗
Hover before you click. Always preview the real URL in the status bar before clicking any link.
🔐
Passphrase > Password. A 4-word passphrase is stronger and easier to remember than complex gibberish.
🔁
Never reuse passwords. One breach can compromise all your accounts — use a password manager.
🎣
Urgency is a red flag. Phishing emails create panic to bypass your judgment — slow down and verify.
🌐
HTTPS ≠ Safe. Padlock only means encrypted connection — attackers use HTTPS too. Check the domain.
